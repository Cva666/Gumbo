Chicken, Sausage, & Shrimp Gumbo

Prep: 20 minutes Cook-time: 6-8 hours

Ingredients

  • Chicken: 2lbs
  • Sausage: 2lbs
  • Shrimp: 1lbs
  • Onion: 1 large cut into 4ths
  • Bell Peppers: 3 sliced length-wise
  • Okra: 1 cup
  • Vegtable Oil: 1 cup
  • Flour: 1 cup
  • Broth: 8 Cups of chicken broth
  • Salt, Pepper, minced garlic, 4 bay leaves

Cooking Steps

  1. Brown your sausage, make sure it gets a slight darkened bark on the outside.
  2. Brown your shrimp, make sure to not over-cook the shrimp or it will get a rubbery texture
  3. Brown your chicken, get the chicken to a nice tan brown color does not need to be fully cooked through
  4. In the oil from your meats, pan-fry your vegatables. Then take them and put them aside. Be sure to keep as much oil in the pan from the cooking process
  5. Add the remaining oil to the pot, allow it to heat up before adding the flour to the mixture.
  6. Stir the flour and oil mixture together. Once the flour is the color of chocolate and smells nutty it is done.
  7. Add the broth, Then the vegtables, meats, and spices.
  8. Allow to cook for 6-8 hours on the stove top on low or in the oven at 300 degrees fahrenheit.

If your oil and flour mixture doesn't want to mix then your oil was too hot
